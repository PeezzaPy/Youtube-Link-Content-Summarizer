What is natural Language Processing?

Natural Languange Processing (NLP) is like teaching computers to understand and work with human languange. It invovles deceloping algorithms and models that enable computers to comprehend, interpret, and generate human languange in a way that's similar to how people do. For instance, NLP helps computers understand the meaning of sentences, extract information from text, and translate language.
