What is Natural Language Processing?

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is like teaching computers to understand and work with human language. It involves developing algorithms and models that enable computers to comprehend, interpret, and generate human language in a way that's similar to how people do. For instance, NLP helps computers understand the meaning of sentences, extract information from text, and translate languages.
